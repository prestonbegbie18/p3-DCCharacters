Red Hood

  • Real Name: Jason Todd
  • Base of Operations: Gotham City
  • Occupation: Anti-hero
  • Main villain: The Joker and Batman's Rogues
  • Weaponry: Dual Handguns and a knife
  • Skills: Excellent in Hand to Hand combat

Backstory

Jason Todd was Batman's second Robin after Dick Grayson left to take on his own crime fighting persona of Nightwing. Jason grew up in a troubled neighborhood. Jason's step-mom, Catherine Todd, died of a drug overdose and his father, Willis Todd, was killed by Two-Face. This forced Jason into a life of crime just to survive. Batman came across Jason while he was stealing the tires off the Batmobile. Seeing potetial in Todd, he took him under his wing as his second Robin. Years later, Jason learned that his real mother, Sheila Haywood, was alive and was captured by the Joker. In an attempt to rescue her, Jason was caught by the Joker and tortured. As Batman arrived on scene, a bomb went off killing Todd and his mother. Years later, a new criminal arrived in Gotham calling himself the Red Hood. After Batman begun his investigation, he quickly learned that the Red Hood was none other than Jason Todd. Bruce learned that his apprentice was resureccted by Ra's Al Ghul. After many confrontations, Todd and Bruce made ammends and Todd was welcomed back into the Bat Family as Red Hood even though Bruce still disagreed with Jason's methods of using firearms and lethal force.
