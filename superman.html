Backstory

When Kal-El was born, Krypton was in the middle of a civil war and the planet was on the verge of blowing up. Kal's father and mother got him off planet before the planet exploded. Kal's pod landed in Kansas, the Kent Farm. Martha and Jonathan Kent took Kal and named him Clark. Throughout his life till his adulthood, his powers began to manifest and Clark began to realize that he was something more than human. There were always incidents that Clark wanted to help stop, but that would put him and the others around him at risk. When he first became Superman, he took a job at The Daily Planet so he could be close to danger without anyone being suspicious. Superman has battled many foes and he remains to be one of the most powerful metahumans in the world.

Superman moments and Fun Facts

  • Superman faced a major defeat by the hands of Doomsday in the Death of Superman
  • In Man of Steel, Superman knew that the only way to protect Earth was to get rid of Zod and his forces by locking them in the Phantom Zone
  • In Batman v Superman, Superman gave his own life to save the world from Doomsday
  • Superman and Batman always have a rivalry about who is the leader of the Justice League
  • Superman is not the only good kryptonian left. He has a cousin, Kara Zor-El, and Krypto the Super Dog
